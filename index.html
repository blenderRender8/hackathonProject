Welcome to the Productivity Resources!

We have a Grading Calculator, Synthetic Division Divider, and a Monthly Meme Competition


Hello! Our project is a website designed as a resource primarily to help students calculate their grades! In addition, we also have a synthetic divison calculator, and we also have a monthly meme competition! Our grade calculator supports up to 5 different sections. It also displays a letter grade at the side when given the ranges for each respective letter grade. Our monthly meme competition feature is not entirely complete, but it features 3 memes that you can have a good laugh at. Our synthetic division calculator is mainly directed towards students taking Algebra 2, and the tool other than the grading calculator we find the most useful.
